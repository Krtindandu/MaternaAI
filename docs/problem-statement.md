MaternaAI

AI-Powered Maternal Mental Health Screening and Emergency Triage Platform

Problem Statement:

Millions of women in rural and underserved communities suffer from postpartum depression and emotional distress that often go undetected due to limited access to mental health professionals. Frontline healthcare workers lack scalable tools to identify high-risk mothers early and connect them with timely care.

MaternaAI empowers ASHA workers and healthcare providers through AI-assisted voice-based screening, risk assessment, and emergency triage, enabling faster identification of vulnerable mothers and improving maternal health outcomes.
